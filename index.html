Frequently Asked Questions

What is the date and location of the event? +

The event will take place on October 15-17, 2026, at the Moscone Center in San Francisco, CA. Virtual attendance options are also available.

Will the sessions be recorded? +

Yes, all keynote and track sessions will be recorded. They will be made available to all registered attendees via the event portal within 48 hours after the event concludes.

Can I get a refund if I can't attend? +

Full refunds are available up to 30 days before the event. After that, you can transfer your ticket to a colleague or switch to a virtual-only pass at no extra cost.

Are there networking opportunities? +

Absolutely. We have dedicated networking lunches, a welcome reception on day one, and designated lounge areas throughout the venue to connect with speakers and peers.
